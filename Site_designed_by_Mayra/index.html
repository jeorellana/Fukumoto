Fukumoto is an izakaya specializing in authentic and traditional Japanese sushi dishes and yakitori. Food is often ordered gradually over several courses, rather than all at once. We have an extensive Japanese sake and beer portfolio. Located in eclectic East Austin, Fukumoto brings to Austin a unique dining experience: Japanese gastropub eatery, incorporating Yakitori (meat skewers) with Edomae sushi (uncommon fish varieties).
